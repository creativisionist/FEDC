CHIC ATMOSPHERE + EXCELLENT COCKTAILS | THE PROMONTORY | MAY 31, 2015 16:59

The Promontory in Hyde Park is a suave venue fulfilling an in-demand niche in an area that otherwise has no options for upscale nights out. The atmosphere is dark and cozy, with a hearth and open-kitchen that makes for a modern environment. The cocktails are fantastic (of particular note is the mixed drink comprising of homemade cream soda and banana rum), and the food is good though somewhat overpriced with limited portion sizes. Overall the venue is a great addition to the neighborhood, which includes a large space on the second floor for live music performances and the monthly salsa night.
